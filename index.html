MiSTer Monitor — touch test (CYD 3.5" resistive)

This build adds support for the resistive touchscreen on your board. The display already works; this time we're testing the touch. It flashes from your browser — nothing to install.

Steps:

  1. Use a computer (not a phone) with Chrome or Edge.
  2. Connect the board with a USB-C data cable (not a charge-only one).
  3. Click Install / Connect, pick the port and wait without touching anything until it finishes.

Firefox and Safari won't work (they lack Web Serial).

After it boots, please tap the screen and tell me:
  • Does the touch respond at all (does anything react when you tap)?
  • If it responds, does it land where you tap, or is it offset / in the wrong place?
  • Do the axes feel correct, or swapped / inverted (you tap one side and it reacts on the other)?

The touch calibration is a first estimate, so some inaccuracy is expected — your feedback is exactly what I need to fine-tune it.
